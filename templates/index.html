7070 Sports Ayodhya Legacy

REVOLUTIONIZING TENNIS CRICKET: From the Sacred Land of Ayodhya to Every Cricket Ground in India.

7070 Sports Ayodhya is not just a brand; it is an emotion. We realized that tennis cricket athletes often lack the professional-grade equipment they truly deserve. To bridge this gap, we launched "India's Most Powerful Double-Pressed Bats," specifically engineered to give every player a professional edge on the field.

Our bats are crafted from premium Grade-1 Kashmir Willow, hand-shaped by master craftsmen to ensure the perfect balance and an explosive ping. By blending modern technology with traditional craftsmanship, we have transformed this Ayodhya-born brand into a symbol of excellence across India.

OUR LEGACY IN NUMBERS:
• 500+ Franchises Nationwide: India's fastest-growing cricket network.

• Endorsed by Legends: High-performance gear used by icons like Jagat Sarkar and Vivek Shelar.

Our vision is to see a 7070 bat roaring in every stadium. We don't just sell bats; we build a player's confidence and help them realize their sporting dreams.
